Data : United State Flight Delay

US Flight Delay is a data set that records flight delays in the United States from various airlines. This data includes various factors that cause delays, including Arrival Delay, Carrier Delay, Late Aircraft Delay, Nas Delay, Total time (minutes) delay caused by the National Aviation System, Security Delay, and Weather Delay. This dataset provides important information about the level of delays that occur in the aviation sector. By looking at this data, it is possible to analyze the patterns of delays that may occur due to these various factors. This information can be useful for passengers, airlines, and other concerned parties to identify the most common causes of delays and take the necessary actions to minimize future delays.

Data : Human Resources in United State

Human Resources (HR) in the United States is a data set that includes information about employees working in various organizations in the country. This data includes information such as the job held by the employee, the location of the workplace, whether they work remotely or at the headquarters, the age of the employee, and so on. Data on the age of employees can also provide insights into the demographics of the workforce in the United States. It can be used to track changes in employee age diversity, retirement trends, and the need to replace future generations of the workforce. This Human Resources dataset has significant value in strategic decision-making and human resource planning across organizations and sectors in the United States. By analyzing this data, companies can understand the structure of their workforce, identify recruitment and training needs, and plan career development in line with business and labor market needs.
